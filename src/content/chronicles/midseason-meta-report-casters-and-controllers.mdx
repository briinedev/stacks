Controllers are easy to notice because their attacks visibly move the elemental economy faster, but the real ladder question is whether those gains are being converted into timely casts or merely stockpiled into neutral positions.

## Current pressure points

- Controller-heavy shells create reliable resource pressure even before damage races become obvious.
- Caster discounts change the threshold where medium-cost spells become routine instead of aspirational.
- Teams that mix generation with disciplined target focus outperform teams that only maximize one dimension.

## What to track in future reports

1. Which openings create the fastest relevant stack colors, not just the largest total stack.
2. Which roles are converting spell windows into confirmed eliminations.
3. Which compositions force defend actions at the highest rate.
